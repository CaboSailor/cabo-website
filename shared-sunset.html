Sunset at Land's End

Land's End at sunset is one of the most photographed and celebrated moments in all of Mexico. As the sun descends toward the Pacific horizon, the sky transforms into a canvas of amber, rose, and violet — with the iconic Arch silhouetted in the foreground.

Our crew positions the boat at the perfect vantage point so every guest has an unobstructed view of this nightly spectacle. It's a moment you'll carry with you long after you leave Cabo.

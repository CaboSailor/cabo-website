Snorkeling at Pelican's Rock

Pelican Rock is one of Cabo's most treasured snorkeling spots, nestled right at the base of the famous Land's End arch. The calm, crystal-clear waters shelter a thriving ecosystem of colorful tropical fish, moray eels, rays, and playful sea lions.

Our crew anchors just meters from the rock, giving you a front-row seat to nature's underwater theater — an experience that keeps guests coming back year after year.

Sunset Sailing at Land's End

Land's End at sunset is one of the most breathtaking sights in all of Mexico. As the sun dips below the Pacific horizon, the famous Arch glows in shades of amber and rose — a moment that stays with you long after the voyage ends. Our private sunset charter anchors at the perfect vantage point to give you and your guests an unobstructed, unforgettable view.

With just your group on board, a professional crew, and an open bar flowing with premium cocktails, this is the definition of a private luxury experience on the water.

Diving at Pelican Rock

Pelican Rock is Cabo's premier dive site, located at the base of the famous Land's End formations. The calm, crystal-clear waters are home to a thriving ecosystem of tropical fish, moray eels, rays, and a playful colony of sea lions that love to interact with divers.

Our expert dive team knows every crevice and coral formation, ensuring you see the very best of what Cabo's underwater world has to offer — from tiny seahorses to massive schools of fish.

Diving at Land's End

Land's End offers a thrilling dive experience at the very tip of the Baja Peninsula, where the Sea of Cortez meets the Pacific Ocean. The dramatic underwater rock formations create a unique habitat for diverse marine life, including large schools of fish, rays, and occasional encounters with larger pelagic species.

The underwater landscape features dramatic walls, swim-throughs, and sandy channels that make every dive an adventure. This site is perfect for certified divers looking for a more challenging and rewarding dive experience.

Snorkeling at Pelican Rock

Pelican Rock is one of the most popular stops on Cabo snorkeling tours. Located inside the protected waters of Cabo San Lucas Bay, just a short 15-minute boat ride from the marina, it offers excellent snorkeling conditions. Sea lions, frigate birds, and pelicans are often seen around this iconic landmark.

With clear water and abundant marine life, Pelican Rock is a fantastic snorkeling spot for both beginners and experienced snorkelers.

Snorkeling at Chileno Bay

Chileno Bay is another excellent snorkeling location in Los Cabos, known for its crystal-clear water and abundant marine life. This protected cove offers calm conditions and great visibility, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced snorkelers.

You can often spot schools of tropical fish and other marine species while exploring the reef. Chileno Bay is one of the highlights of our private yacht snorkeling cruises.

Snorkeling at Santa María Bay

Santa María Bay is one of the most beautiful and protected snorkeling spots in Los Cabos. This horseshoe-shaped bay features calm, clear turquoise waters and a sandy bottom, making it ideal for swimming and snorkeling.

The area is part of a marine sanctuary, often filled with colorful fish and vibrant marine life. Santa María Bay is a favorite stop during our private yacht cruises along the Cabo coastline.
